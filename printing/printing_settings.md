# Printing settings for zebrafish molds

These settings were generated and validated in PrusaSlicer 2.8.1 for an Original Prusa i3 MK3S/MK3S+ printer using Prusament PLA (1.75 mm, Jet Black).

---

## General
- **Printer technology:** FDM (FFF)
- **Printer model:** Prusa i3 MK3S/MK3S+
- **Nozzle diameter:** 0.4 mm
- **Layer height:** 0.10 mm (high-detail setting)
- **Extrusion width:** 0.45 mm

---

## Temperatures
- **Nozzle temperature:** 215 °C (first layer and subsequent layers)
- **Bed temperature:** 60 °C (first layer and subsequent layers)

---

## Speeds
- **Perimeters:** 30 mm/s  
- **External perimeters:** 15 mm/s  
- **Small perimeters:** 15 mm/s  
- **Infill:** 60 mm/s  
- **Solid infill:** 80 mm/s  
- **Top solid infill:** 40 mm/s  
- **Bridges:** 25 mm/s  
- **First layer:** 20 mm/s  

---

## Perimeters & shell
- **Perimeters:** 2  
- **Top solid layers:** 8  
- **Bottom solid layers:** 5  
- **Seam position:** Aligned  

---

## Infill
- **Density:** 5%  
- **Pattern:** Gyroid  
- **Overlap:** 25%  

---

## Supports
- **Style:** Organic supports  
- **Pattern:** Honeycomb  
- **Threshold angle:** 50°  
- **Contact distance:** 0.2 mm  
- **Interface layers:** 2  
- **Speed:** 50 mm/s  

---

## Cooling
- **Fan:** 100% after layer 4  
- **Disable fan on first layers:** Yes (first 3 layers)  

---

## Retraction
- **Length:** 0.8 mm  
- **Lift (Z-hop):** 0.4 mm  
- **Speed:** 35 mm/s  

---

## Adhesion
- **Brim:** Enabled (5 mm width, outer only)  
- **Skirt:** 1 loop, 2 mm distance  

---

## Post-processing
- After printing, sand the molds to remove roughness.  
- Apply a thin coat of two-part epoxy and allow to cure for 24 h before use.  

---

## Notes
- These settings prioritize surface quality and fine detail for the narrow mold slots.  
- Printing at 0.10 mm layer height ensures sharper cavity edges, though it increases print time.  
- 100% infill can be used if extra rigidity is desired, but 5% gyroid with multiple perimeters was sufficient in our tests.
